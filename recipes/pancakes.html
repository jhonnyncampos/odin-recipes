Pancakes

Description:

Try this delicious and easy pancake recipe for a tasty breakfast treat. Made with simple ingredients and ready in just minutes, these pancakes are sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a seperate mixing bowl, beat the egg, then stir in the milk and vegetable oil.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, and stir until the batter is smooth.
  4. Heat a large skillet over medium heat, and lightly grease with cooking spray or butter.
  5. Pour the batter onto the skillet in small circles, and cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes. Flip with a spatula, and cook until the other side is golden brown.
  6. Serve the pancakes with your favorite toppings, such as syrup, butter, fruit, or whipped cream. Enjoy!
