Tacos

Description:

This tasty taco recipe is a fun and flavorful way to enjoy a classic mexican dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small flour or corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 diced tomato
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 avocado, mashed
  • Sour cream and salsa, for serving
  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until it's browned and fully cooked.
  2. Drain any excess fat from the skillet, then add the taco seasoning and mix it into the beef. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the seasoning is fully incorporated and the beef is heated through.
  3. Warm the tortillas according to the package instructions.
  4. Assemble the tacos by spooning the seasond beef onto the tortillas, then topping with lettuce, cheese, tomato, onion, and avocado.
  5. Serve the tacos immediately, with sour cream and salsa on the side if desired.
