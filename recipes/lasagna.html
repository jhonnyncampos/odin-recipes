Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta noodles interspersed with a mixture of meat and vegetables, and covered with a rich tomato or cream sauce and melted cheese.

The dish is typically baked in the oven, allowing the flavors to meld and the cheese to become golden and bubbly. Lasagna is typically served as a hearty main course, and can be paired with a salad or other side dishes for a complete meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon sugar
  • 8 lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesean
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the beef is browned. Drain off any excess fat.
  2. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, parsley, salt, black pepper, basil, oregano, and sugar. Simmer for at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  3. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles in the boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente. Drain the noodles, and rinse them under cold water.
  4. In a small bowl, mix the ricotta cheese and egg together.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  6. To assemble the lasagna, spread a small amount of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 4 noodles over the sauce. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, and sprinkle with half of the parmesean cheese. Top with a third of the remaining meat sauce, and half of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of noodles and the remaining meat sauce. Sprinkle the top with the remaining mozzarella.
  7. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  8. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil, and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the top is lightly browned and the lasagna is heated through. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
